Sai Seva Sangh strongly believes that education has the power to transform lives. We want to ensure that every child, regardless of their social and economic background, has access to quality education, enjoys learning and makes progress towards discovering true potential and realizing their dreams

OUR PHILOSOPHY

VISION :

To empower a girl child through integral education focused on academic and non-academic learning, nurturing confidence, self-esteem and moulding them as ideal citizens devoted to the service of society. To empower destitute women by providing shelter and skill development programmes, thus enhancing their financial sustenance.

MISSION : :

To enable a brighter future for girl children & women from low-income communities by providing them with the desired knowledge, skills & values and support, to achieve their true potential.

Impact Stories

Student beneficiary – Navaneetha

1. Navaneetha’s father passed away due to AIDS when she was a young child
2. Her mother supports the family by working as a daily wage laborer
3. Navaneetha is one of four children in the family
4. Due to financial constraints, her mother couldn’t afford her education
5. Navaneetha’ s mother enrolled her in Sai Seva Sangh when she was in the 1st standard
6. Despite the odds, Navaneetha successfully completed her B.A. degree
7. She now serves as an intern at our school, sharing her knowledge and experiences with her younger sisters
In 2024, Navaneetha is set to pursue post-graduate studies, continuing her educational journey

Student beneficiary – Neha

1. Neha tragically lost both her parents at a young age in distressing circumstances
2. She is the youngest among her three sisters in the family
3. Sai Seva Sangh provides support to Neha and her sisters
4. Neha is an incredibly dynamic and active participant in various sports events
5. She excels as a basketball player and holds a black belt in karate, with numerous medals at district and state levels.
6. She is a dedicated member of the NCC and has undergone training in several self-defense programs.
Currently, Neha is in her first year of pursuing a BSc degree with a focus on mathematics, statistics, and computer science.
